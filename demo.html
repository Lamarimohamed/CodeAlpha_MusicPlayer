How to Use

  • Click the Play Button - Start playing the current song with beautiful animations
  • Use Navigation Buttons - Skip to next/previous songs with smooth transitions
  • Drag the Progress Bar - Click anywhere on the progress bar to seek to that position
  • Adjust Volume - Use the volume slider or drag the volume handle for precise control
  • Browse Playlist - Click any song in the playlist to play it immediately
  • Keyboard Shortcuts - Use Space, Arrow keys, and M for quick control
